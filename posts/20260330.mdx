I'm 4-day away from my 35th birthday.

Morning metro rides really gave me a chance to reflect and gather thoughts on things I want to do, rather than consuming contents being generated by other people.

Similar to ten thousands other human beings, I've always wanted to write a journal on my own, especially when I had random ideas that I was afraid of being lost through the busy days. However, it was always easier to procrastinate my way through it. It's getting more tempting now that Bơ and Xoài have come to my life, for whom I wish my thoughts could reach when they are mature enough. Heck who knows in that far future, human can use journal to predict and reconstruct someone's mind (I tried not to insert the word "AI", as it's a more of a buzzword now than ever, or will ever be).

I'll keep this journal as raw as it could be, including my wrong uses of words or grammar. I'm not writing it to impress anyone anyway, which is quite a rare chance in this high-expectation world.
